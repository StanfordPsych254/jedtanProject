Thank you for participating in this study. The purpose of this study was to explore the role of object persistence in spatial navigation. Observations made from this study have potential application in areas such as smartphone or application vision and design. In this study we asked participants to attempt to traverse grids of elements one element at a time with grid movement transitions designed to represent a persistent grid of objects (or not a persistent grid of objects).
